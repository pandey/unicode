Documents

This repository lists the documents associated with my script and character encoding projects. These documents contain detailed histories of writing systems, descriptions of orthographies, examples of usage, and encoding models, and implementation details. All of my contributions are based upon original research that I have performed in archives, in the field, and through native user collaboration. In several cases, my document may be the only English language resource for a given script or character.
