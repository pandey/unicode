A grant from the Henry Luce Foundation to SEI made possible a meeting with experts of Mongolian scripts, held October 15-17, 2015 in Tokyo. The following reports on the Soyombo and Zanabazar Square script were produced in relation to the meeting:

  • Response to Mongolian and Japanese comments on Soyombo and Zanabazar Square (L2/15-094)
  • Revised code chart and names list for Zanabazar Square (L2/15-246)
  • Revised code chart and names list for Soyombo (L2/15-247)
  • Proposal to encode JIHVAMULIYA and UPADHMANIYA for Soyombo (L2/15-331)
  • Comments on L2/15-249 regarding Soyombo and Zanabazar Square punctuation (L2/15-336)
