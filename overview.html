Overview

I’ve added 28 scripts (with more 2 pending), 4 number systems, 3 emoji, and several other characters to the Unicode standard.

I’m actively working include 25+ additional scripts and conducting research on 100+ more scripts.

I’m also working on proposals for new emoji.
